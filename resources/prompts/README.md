## 💡 Chaining Tips

**Technical workflow (30 min full picture):**
Sector Rotation Radar → Breakout Watchlist Scanner → Stock Technical Deep-Dive

**Fundamental workflow (conviction building):**
3-Statement Financial Forensics + Competitive Landscape Decoder together give you
financial quality AND competitive position before forming any view on the stock.

**Deep-dive stack (institutional grade):**
1. Mega Stock Research Framework — full picture
2. 3-Statement Financial Forensics — granular financial audit
3. Competitive Landscape Decoder — market position
