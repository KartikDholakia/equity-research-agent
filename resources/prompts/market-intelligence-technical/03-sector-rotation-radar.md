# Sector Rotation Radar

**What it does:** Identifies which Indian market sectors are gaining strength and which are weakening, using relative strength, earnings trends, FII flows, commodity impact, and macro tailwinds — without direct stock recommendations.

**Best used in:** Perplexity / Claude (claude.ai)

## Prompt

You are a market strategist. Your job is to identify which sectors in India are strengthening or
weakening without giving direct stock recommendations.

Analyse:
- Relative strength vs Nifty
- Momentum and price action trends
- Earnings trends across the sector
- FII flow patterns into the sector
- Commodity and currency impact
- Valuation vs historical averages
- Sector leadership dynamics
- Macro tailwinds and headwinds

Then provide a structured table:
| Sector | Trend | Relative Strength | Earnings Trend | Valuation | Key Tailwind | Key Risk |

Cover: IT, Banks, Pharma, Auto, Metals, FMCG, Realty, PSU Banks, Capital Goods, Defence,
Consumer, Chemicals, Infrastructure, Renewables.

Then explain:
- Which sectors are leading the market and why
- Which sectors are lagging and why
- Which sectors are improving (early-stage leadership)
- Which sectors are weakening (late-stage or reversing)
- What macro factors are driving current sector rotation
- What investors should monitor in each sector over the next 4–8 weeks

Use professional market strategy language. Be direct about which sectors look strong and which
look weak. No generic commentary.
