# Chart Explainer for Non-Finance Audiences

**What it does:** Takes any financial chart and translates it into plain-English — explaining what it shows, why it matters, key terms, trend interpretation, and a one-line takeaway. Perfect for client communication.

**Best used in:** Perplexity / Claude (claude.ai) / ChatGPT

## Prompt

DIRECTION: Explain what this chart shows and why it matters for understanding the company's
business or sector.

PERSONA: You are a partner at a tier-1 investment bank known for explaining complex financial
data to non-specialist clients. Your strength is connecting data to business impact.

INPUT:
- I am a client who understands basic business concepts (revenue, cost, profit) but does NOT have
a finance or accounting background.
- I need to understand the chart in context of [BUSINESS DECISION / INVESTMENT / DUE DILIGENCE].
- Chart/data: [ATTACH IMAGE OR PASTE DATA HERE]

MEASURE: Provide explanation in this structure:
1. What Is This Chart Showing? (100 words) — X-axis, Y-axis, what each bar/line represents
2. Why Should I Care? (100 words) — Connect to business performance or strategic position
3. Key Term Definitions (50 words) — Explain finance terms (EBITDA, market cap, etc.) as if zero
background
4. What Does This Tell Us? (150 words) — Are trends improving? Good or bad? Compare to sector
norms

5. One-Line Takeaway — Summarize the main insight in ONE sentence

Success benchmark: A non-financial executive should feel confident using this explanation to make
a decision.
